Target audience

Our Chamber of Commerce targets businesses, professionals and none professionals in the community. Our goal is to provide a supportive network for growth and success through events and different resources. Our Chamber is dedicated to helping people in a range from 18 to 60 years old, the one who is just looking to find an event or to know what the weather will be, or the one who is looking to find businesses opportunities and a place to find a community to make contacts, join us to connect with others, find opportunities, stay informed on the latest events, and grow your network.
