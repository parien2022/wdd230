-------General information-------

The creation of the Uruguayan Chamber of Commerce marks an important milestone in the economic development of the Montevideo city. By bringing together diverse businesses and fostering collaboration, the chamber strives to create a thriving business community that contributes to the prosperity and progress of Montevideo, making it an attractive destination for local and international investment.
